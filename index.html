Ranking King Krule's albums

Here is a list of my favorite King Krule albums, ranked in order of personal preference.
Along with each album comes a short description. I'm using Genius as my source.

  1. THE OOZ

    The OOZ is the second album by King Krule, released on October 13, 2017.
    With this record, I felt I changed my sound a bit. I used more distortion. I dried out my vocals a lot.

  2. Man Alive!

    Man Alive! is the third studio record by King Krule, following his last full-length LP The OOZ released back in 2017.
    The album features artwork by Archy Marshall’s brother Jack, paintings by their dad Adam, and photographies by Archy Marshall’s wife Charlotte Patmore.

  3. Space Heavy

    Space Heavy is the fourth album by King Krule, released on June 9, 2023.
    Many of the songs premiered during King Krule’s “SHHHHH! Tour”.
